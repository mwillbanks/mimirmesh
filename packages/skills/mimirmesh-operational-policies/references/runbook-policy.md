# Runbook Policy

Runbooks track operator procedures, not generic design commentary.

- Update a runbook when rollout steps, recovery steps, health checks, troubleshooting, or escalation paths change.
- Do not create or update runbooks for changes that are purely internal and invisible to operators.
- Tie runbook content to the real runtime and deployment model.
- If the procedure is only a feature explanation, update `docs/features/` or `docs/architecture/` instead.
- Prefer a narrow, concrete procedure over a broad autogenerated narrative.
