# Closeout Policy

Closeout is complete only when all artifacts affected by the change are synchronized.

- Code, tests, and validation must agree with the delivered behavior.
- Architecture docs, feature docs, and runbooks must be updated when their thresholds are crossed.
- ADRs must be updated or added when the ADR policy requires it.
- Reports and examples must not point to removed tool names, removed skill names, or legacy draft structures.
- Obsolete draft skill files must be removed once the canonical bundle exists.
- Final output should identify what changed, what was validated, and any residual gap that could not be closed in the current session.
